Dark-sector resources

Some useful introductions:

  • "Lectures on the Geometric Anatomy of Theoretical Physics by Dr Frederic Schuller is a great introduction to applying mathematical concepts in physics. Covers basic differential geometry, Lie groups and their algebras, and representation theory.
  • These notes by Josh Kirklin, for the 2015 "Symmetries, Particles and Fields" Part III course taught by Nick Dorey.
  • David Tongs notes for literally anything, but my favourites are QFT, GR and String Theory.

About Me

I grew up in Newtownabbey, a suburb of the city of Belfast. As a child, I loved palaeontology; I knew the names of dinosaurs, what they ate, the length of their tails etc. Who could ever have predicted that one day I really would be digging up fossils - QCD papers from the 80s (and like real fossils, they can teach you a lot about the present).
